World biggest employers.

Employment is one of the biggest issue in the world. Nearer everyone in the world want to be employed by a good company that pays well. This being the case, job demand is very high and supply is low leading to many people remaining unemployed which increases poverty. Governments all over the world are increasingly being involved in taking measures to reduce unemployment in their jurisdictions. This is mostly in third world countries whose citizens are poor for lack of employment.

Being employed means one is getting paid for working for an organization or a company or another individual. Employed person is paid via cash on a periodic basis. The period is as per the employment contract which vary but mostly the period is usually a month.

The employment contract is an agreement entered between the employer and the employee which serves as guide in the relationship between the employer and employee. It states the payment amount, the period one is employed, the job description, validity of contract, probational period among other things.

One is employed through application. Application can be through various means such emails formal letters, etc. Employment can be classified into five main categories. This include permanent employment, contractual employment, casual employment, part time employment and self employment.

Permanent employment is the most common and preferred type of employment by many people because of the income security and various benefits it comes with. Self employment is also preferred by many people because of the independence it comes with. One becomes your own boss and doesn't have to share income you earn.

Employment is not only beneficial to the employee since it is important to many other parties in an country. The government benefits from it employed citizens through taxation. Living standards of a community are improved by employment. High level of employment means happiness and growth for all involved.
