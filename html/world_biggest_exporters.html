The World Biggest Exporters.

Exports refer to goods or services produced by a country and consumed by another country. Exports can be done by an individual or a company. As long as production was done in a different country to which it is being consumed, that export. Export a major item of international trade. Seller of this types of good is known as the exporters and the buyer is known as the importer.

Exporting of goods and services is preferred by companies since it increases a company's market meaning more revenue for a company. A country will export goods to gain revenue. Countries buying from another country benefit by saving the cost of producing the goods and services themselves. Exports therefore benefits all parties involved.

The method used in exports may include hand delivery, shipping, selling via internet, mailing, telephone conversation etc. As long as the good was produced in a different country and consumed in a another, the means it took to reach there doesn't matter to qualify to be an export. Thus, why exports are such a common phenomenon.

There are various factors that are barriers to exports. Different countries have different laws concerning consumption of goods and services. This may lead to confusion when dealing with goods and services in a foreign country. Restrictive business where a country has restricted goods from another country also affects exports of goods and services in a big way.

Although, as discussed above parties involved in exports all benefit in one way or another, exports to have disadvantages. This may include things such as unnecessary competition where you find goods exported creating competition to locally produced products for the country that imported the goods. Cost involved in exporting is quite high. Transport, regulatory authorities involved etc. Exports are not suitable for all types of products especially perishable products.
