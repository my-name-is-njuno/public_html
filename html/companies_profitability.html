World's Biggest Profits Ever Reported

Profits is the surplus of revenue on expenses. That is the leftovers of income after ones expenses are cleared. For a company or an individual in business to be profitable, the revenues must exceed the expenses of a particular period. It is usually the main goal of all businesses.

In the world we live today, very few companies make super abnormal profits. Most of them make normal profits while a few others make losses. This is influenced by many factors which can be grouped into two major categories -: external and internal factors.

Internal factors usually are factors that a company is able to control. This factors include direct costs, management styles, type of good produced, brand image, marketing styles and approaches, employees etc. The company is able to control employees since it is responsible for hiring, management decisions are under the company's control, the type of goods to produce is a company's decision etc.

External factors on the other hand are factors that a company has no control over. This may include factors such as government regulations, political stability, weather changes, environment occurrences such as earthquakes, competition in the market place:- the list is endless. A company doesn't have control on tax regulations in place in an economy, but they have to follow the set rules, same case for decisions made by it competitors.

With this in mind, companies have to utilize their knowledge to mix the various factors of production in a way that will lead to profits. They will have to hire skilled employees to be able to achieve their goals in proper management, they will also have to adhere to set rules and regulations to avoid being on the wrong side of the law. That is a company has to come up with a plan to mix up the the various factors so as to make profits.

In the world, some companies have implemented their plans in mixing the various factors of production to end up making the highest profits ever reported. Below is a list of the top fifty companies that have reported the highest profits in the periods indicated. They are -:
